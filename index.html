EDITORIAL METHOD

Signal, not hype.

01

Primary sources first

Metadata is gathered from OpenAlex, arXiv, and Europe PMC. Preprints are labeled so they are never presented as peer-reviewed findings.

02

Transparent ranking

The signal score combines recency, evidence language, citation activity, access status, and cross-domain relevance. It is not a truth score.

03

No quantum mysticism shortcut

Quantum-consciousness claims are surfaced only when the publication itself connects the fields, and the evidence level stays visible.

This is a discovery tool, not peer review, medical guidance, or proof that a reported result is correct. Read the original work before drawing conclusions.
